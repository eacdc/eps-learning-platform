Account & data deletion

EPS Digital Learning (Android app)
Operator: CDC Printers
Last updated: 24 April 2026

This page explains how to ask us to delete your EPS Digital Learning account and the personal data tied to it. You can use it when following a link from the Google Play store listing.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address registered on your app account (or clearly identify the account if you no longer have access to that inbox). We use this to help confirm the request is yours.

Email: ea@cdcprinters.com

Suggested subject line: Account deletion request — EPS Digital Learning

Include in your message:

  1. That you want your EPS Digital Learning account deleted.
  2. The email address or username you used to register or sign in (so we can find the correct account).
  3. If you signed in with Google, say so and give the Google account email you used.
  4. Any other detail that helps us match your account (for example the full name on the profile), if asked.

We may reply to verify your identity before we delete the account. Please send the request only for your own account, or with appropriate authority (for example a parent or guardian for a child’s account, where applicable).

What we delete

After we confirm your request and verify identity where needed, we intend to delete or anonymize account and profile data we hold for that account in our systems, including for example: account identifiers, name, email, phone if provided, profile image if stored on our servers, and learning activity tied to that account (such as chat history, scores, progress, and subscriptions associated with that account), subject to the exceptions below.

What we may keep and for how long

  • Legal and security: We may retain certain information if the law requires it, or for a limited time to resolve disputes, enforce our terms, or detect abuse. When retention is required, we keep it only as long as necessary for those purposes.
  • Backups: Data in routine backups may persist for a short period until those backups rotate or expire; active use of your account will stop once deletion is processed.
  • Aggregated or de-identified data: We may keep statistics that do not identify you.
  • Third parties: If you used Sign in with Google, Google holds data under Google’s policies. Managing or deleting your Google account is done in your Google account settings; we delete the link between our service and your account as part of closing your app account on our side.

When we respond

We aim to acknowledge requests within a reasonable time and complete deletion or tell you if we need more information. Complex requests may take longer.

Other privacy information

For how we collect and use data more generally, see our Privacy Policy for EPS Digital Learning (use the same policy link as in the app or on your store listing).
